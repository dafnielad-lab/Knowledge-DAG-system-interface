$\cos(\alpha - \beta) = \cos(\alpha + (-\beta)) = \cos\alpha\cos(-\beta) - \sin\alpha\sin(-\beta) = \cos\alpha\cos\beta + \sin\alpha\sin\beta$. $\blacksquare$
