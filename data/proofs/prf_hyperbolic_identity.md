$\cosh^2 x - \sinh^2 x = \frac{(e^x + e^{-x})^2 - (e^x - e^{-x})^2}{4} = \frac{(2e^x)(2e^{-x})}{4} = \frac{4}{4} = 1$ (לפי זהות $a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)$). $\blacksquare$
